Dr. Bruno Lopes Dalmazo

Short Bio: Bruno Dalmazo received his Ph.D. degree in Information Science and Technology from the University of Coimbra in 2018. He completed his Master's degree in Computer Science in 2011 at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul, Brazil. Bruno also received a Bachelors degree in Computer Science in 2008 from the Federal University of Santa Maria, Brazil. Currently, he is a postdoc research fellow at the Institute of Informatics of the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul. His primary research interests involve network traffic prediction, as well as security and privacy in cloud computing environments.
